Eat for the Planet

Our food choices have a profound impact on the environment. By making conscious decisions about what we eat and how it's produced, we can reduce our carbon footprint, support sustainable agriculture, and protect our planet's resources. This page explores key considerations for building a more sustainable diet, from choosing organic and locally sourced foods to minimizing your consumption of processed and packaged items.

Consider the Source

What you eat doesn't just impact your health, it also impacts the health of our planet. Choose organic fruits and vegetables and pasture-raised meats.

Drinks Matter Too

Even what we drink can have a big impact. Use a refillable water bottle instead of using plastic. Choose organic wine to ensure you're not drinking chemicals.
